Here's a list to make cookies

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Sift
    • Flour
    • baking soda
    • salt
    • Sugar
    • & cocoa
    in a bowl
  3. Mix
    • Eggs
    • Butter
    in another bowl
  4. Combine bowl mixtures
  5. Butter Baking tray
  6. Dollop cookie mix with sufficient spacing
  7. bake in oven for 20 minutes
  8. Bring tray out and let cool
  9. Enjoy!
